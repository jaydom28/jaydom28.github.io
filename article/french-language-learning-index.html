French Pronouns

Verb Conjugations

These three verbs are irregular in both Spanish and French, may be important to learn these verbs when learning any romance language.

These verbs are irregular:
To be - etre
To have - avoir
To go - aller
To do - faire

In French there are three verb endings for regular verbs, -ir, -er and -re.

Verb with an -re ending, perdre - to lose

Pronoun Conjugation Pronoun Conjugation
Je perds Nous perdons
Tu perds Vous perdez
Il / Elle perd Ils / Elles perdent
